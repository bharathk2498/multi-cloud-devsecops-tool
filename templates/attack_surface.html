ASM Best Practices for Multi-Cloud Environments
Discovery & Inventory
  • Implement continuous asset discovery across all cloud accounts
  • Maintain centralized asset inventory with metadata
  • Use cloud-native tools for resource enumeration
  • Monitor shadow IT and unauthorized resources
  • Track external-facing services and endpoints
Risk Assessment & Monitoring
  • Prioritize internet-facing assets for assessment
  • Implement automated vulnerability scanning
  • Monitor for configuration drift and changes
  • Establish risk scoring and prioritization
  • Integrate with threat intelligence feeds
